Our Legendary
Butter Chicken

A timeless classic, and a cornerstone of our menu. Our authentic Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani) features tender, charcoal-grilled chicken pieces simmered to perfection in our signature velvety tomato-cream sauce. Rich, flavorful, and made with love.

Our Master Chef’s Secret Recipe

Key Ingredients:

  • Chicken: Boneless thigh pieces (800g), yogurt marinade, tandoori spices.
  • Sauce Base: Ripe tomatoes (1kg), ginger-garlic paste, cashews (50g, soaked).
  • Richness: Fresh cream (100ml), butter (50g), olive oil.
  • Spices: Kasoori Methi (dried fenugreek leaves), Garam Masala, Kashmiri Chili powder, cardamom.
  • Balance: Sugar/honey (1 tsp), salt to taste.

Simple Method (Restaurant Style):

  1. Marinate: Coat chicken in yogurt and spices for at least 4 hours. Grill in a tandoor or pan-sear until charred.
  2. Sauce: Sauté ginger-garlic paste, add tomatoes and cashews. Cook until mushy. Blend and strain for a smooth base.
  3. Build: Heat butter and oil. Add spices, then the strained tomato base. Simmer.
  4. Combine: Add grilled chicken to the sauce. Stir in cream and Kasoori Methi. Cook for another 5 minutes on low.
  5. Serve: Garnish with cream and coriander. Best served with Naan or Jeera Rice.
